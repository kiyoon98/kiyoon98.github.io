Design Your Own Project

Don't like any of the other project ideas? Design your own. You are welcome to suggest a project of your choosing to your instructor for approval.

Want to model or simulate something? Want to examine large amounts of text? Have your own ideas for a project? Proposals for your own project are welcome. However, please note that they need to incorporate several of the topics below before they will be approved.

  • Getting input from the user
  • Loops
  • Conditional Statements
  • Mathematical Operations
  • File Reading and Writing
